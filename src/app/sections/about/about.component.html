About Us

When word spread that a farmer's market was starting in town, this hobby baker knew it was time to open a small business. We are a home bakery, officially referred to in legal parlance as a Cottage Food Operator; our home is little and red. And so, we became Red Cottage Bakery!

We favor classic desserts and familiar flavors. Our menu is a blend of heritage recipes passed down through generations, re-creations of nostalgia desserts and enduring favorites from well-worn books. We bake with the seasons, using premium and local ingredients at every opportunity.

Baking brings joy. Thank you for sharing in our fun.

Betsy Kroll, owner/baker
